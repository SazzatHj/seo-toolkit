# Comprehensive SEO Suites & Market Intelligence

These platforms are all-in-one SEO suites used for keyword research, competitor analysis, backlink tracking, and site audits.

---

### 1. Semrush
*   **How to Use:** 
    1. Enter your competitor's domain into the **Domain Overview** search bar to see their organic traffic and top-ranking keywords.
    2. Use the **Keyword Magic Tool** to find low-competition long-tail keywords.
    3. Run a **Site Audit** to get a step-by-step checklist of technical issues on your website.
*   **Pricing:** Freemium (10 free requests per day with limitations) / Premium.

### 2. Ahrefs
*   **How to Use:**
    1. Paste any URL into **Site Explorer** to inspect its backlink profile and domain authority.
    2. Use **Keywords Explorer** to analyze Keyword Difficulty (KD) and click metrics.
    3. Use the free **Ahrefs Webmaster Tools (AWT)** by verifying your site ownership to get free ongoing technical health audits.
*   **Pricing:** Paid (Offers excellent free standalone tools like Free Backlink Checker and Keyword Rank Checker).

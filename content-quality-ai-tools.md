# Content Writing, Plagiarism, & AI Detection Tools

Tools dedicated to optimizing content readability, ensuring originality, and detecting AI-generated text.

---

### 1. SmallSEOTools
*   **How to Use:** A Swiss Army knife for quick tasks. Navigate to their website to use free individual modules like Text Analysis, Image Resizing, Word Counter, or Domain Authority Checker.
*   **Pricing:** Free (Ad-supported).

### 2. ZeroGPT
*   **How to Use:** Paste your written text into the input box and click "Detect Text". It analyzes the perplexity and burstiness of sentences to tell you what percentage of the text was written by AI (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.).
*   **Pricing:** Free / Premium.

### 3. Duplichecker & Plagiarism Checker
*   **How to Use:** Paste your blog post draft (up to 1,000 words per search) into the box, click "Check Plagiarism", and it will scan the entire web to ensure your text is 100% original before publishing.
*   **Pricing:** Free.

### 4. Hemingway App
*   **How to Use:** Paste your article text directly into the web editor. It highlights complex sentences in red/yellow, passive voice in green, and gives you a overall **Readability Grade Level** (Aim for Grade 6 to 8 for best SEO performance).
*   **Pricing:** 100% Free Web Version.
